# Japan Ski Resort Guide

![日本滑雪場指南預覽圖](public/images/og-image.png)

After falling in love with skiing, I decided to go skiing in Japan at least once every year. But every time I planned a trip, researching was a hassle: I kept jumping back and forth between the same websites and ended up making piles of notes.

After doing this for years, I figured I might as well build a website myself. It is something I can use, share with others, and hopefully help everyone who loves skiing.

Japan Ski Resort Guide is a database of ski resorts in Japan. It mainly organizes ski resorts across Japan, ski travel areas, prefecture highlights, course information, and official reference links in Traditional Chinese.

## 🏂 How To Use It

- Find candidate resorts for your next Japan ski trip.
- Compare resorts that can fit into the same travel area.
- Quickly understand resort highlights, target users, course style, and official information.
- Narrow down options by prefecture, region, tags, or keywords.
- Check ski course videos before deciding where to go.

## 🗺️ Pages

| Page | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Home | Starts from Japan ski regions, with list and map-style browsing. |
| Resort List | Lists collected resorts with keyword, prefecture, and tag filters. |
| Resort Detail | Covers resort highlights, basic information, season hours, tickets, access, course overview, and extra references. |
| Course Detail | Groups ski courses by difficulty and includes length, slope, notes, and related videos when available. |
| Region Pages | Organize resorts by major Japan regions such as Hokkaido, Tohoku, and Kanto-Koshinetsu. |
| Prefecture Index | Provides entry points by Japanese prefecture. |
| Prefecture Pages | Introduce each prefecture's ski character and related resorts. |
| Ski Area Index | Groups common ski travel areas such as Yuzawa, Hakuba, and Myoko. |
| Ski Area Pages | Explain area character, travel hub context, nearby resort relationships, and featured resorts. |

## ✨ Features

- Keyword search: Find resorts by Traditional Chinese, English, or partial resort names.
- Filters: Narrow results by prefecture and tags.
- Region browsing: Start from a broad Japan ski region before choosing a resort.
- Prefecture browsing: Compare resorts and ski characteristics by prefecture.
- Ski area browsing: Browse by practical ski trip areas.
- Course videos: Related videos are connected to individual course entries where possible.

## ❄️ Snow And Weather

The site currently focuses on stable resort information, regional introductions, and course data. Snow conditions and weather information will be added again when the future snow season starts.

## 📚 Data Sources

This project mainly refers to official and publicly available information, then rewrites and organizes it for Taiwanese skiers.

- [Japan National Tourism Organization / Travel Japan](https://www.japan.travel/tw/)
- [Travel Japan Ski Resort Directory](https://www.japan.travel/tw/travel-directory/%E6%BB%91%E9%9B%AA%E5%A0%B4/)
- [Travel Japan Destinations](https://www.japan.travel/tw/destinations/)
- [Weathernews Ski & Snow](https://weathernews.jp/s/ski/)
- Official ski resort websites, official trail maps, ticket pages, business information pages, and access information pages.
- Public ski videos and travel articles used as supplementary references for course feel, area character, and trip planning.
