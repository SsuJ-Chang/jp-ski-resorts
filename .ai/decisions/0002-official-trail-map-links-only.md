# Decision: Official Trail Map Links Only

## Status

Accepted

## Date

2026-05-19

## Context

Trail maps are important for ski trip planning, but official resort maps and PDFs may be copyrighted.

## Decision

The MVP will link to official trail map pages and official trail map PDFs instead of downloading, copying, or redistributing trail map files.

## Reasons

- Avoids copyright and licensing issues.
- Keeps the repository small.
- Preserves a clear source path to official resort information.

## Consequences

- Users may leave the site to inspect full trail maps.
- Some official links may change over time and will need checking.
- Future redistribution requires explicit license confirmation.
